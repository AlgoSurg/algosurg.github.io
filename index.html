    What we do

    We are developing a web-based software for orthopedic surgeons to accurately plan deformity and replacement surgeries in 3D and to get Patient Specific Instrumentation (PSI) within minutes, using only two 2D X-ray images. The 3D surgery plan will help surgeons to take accurate surgical decisions and the PSI (which will be 3D printed) will enable surgeon to make the plan into reality.
