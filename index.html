    What we do

    ALgoSurgTM team is developing a cloud based software for orthopedic surgeons to accurately plan deformity correction and knee replacement surgeries in 3D view and to get Patient Specific Instrumentation (PSI) design within minutes, using only two 2D X-ray images. The 3D virtual surgery plan will enable surgeons to make accurate surgical decisions and the PSI (to be 3D printed) will help to execute the plan, resulting an accurate surgical outcome.
